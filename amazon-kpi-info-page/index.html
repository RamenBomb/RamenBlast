Amazon KPI Dashboard App

This is a private application developed and operated by a single Amazon seller. The app connects to the Amazon Selling Partner API (SP-API) to retrieve performance data related to product sales, advertising, and customer metrics.

The goal of the app is to track and visualize key performance indicators (KPIs) such as TACoS, ROAS, conversion rates, sessions, and net profit, per ASIN.

No Amazon customer information is stored or processed. Data is retrieved solely for internal use by the developer on their own seller account.

All Amazon Information is stored securely using Google Firebase services (Firestore database), and access is restricted to the developer only. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

This app does not share any Amazon Information with outside parties.
